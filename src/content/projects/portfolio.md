Developed a calendar-view dashboard for managers to visualize team absences across time, enabling at-a-glance leave planning and reducing manual coordination effort within the organization.
Implemented bcrypt-based password encryption for all user accounts, hardening authentication security, and ensuring compliance with best practices for credential storage in a multi-user system.
